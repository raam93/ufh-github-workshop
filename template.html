About

I'm a second-year graduate student studying urban heat islands and their impact on low-income neighbourhoods. My research combines satellite imagery analysis with community interviews to understand how green infrastructure can reduce temperature disparities across Toronto. I'm passionate about using data to advocate for more equitable city planning.
